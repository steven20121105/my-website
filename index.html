About Penguin-Chip

Founded in 2024, Penguin-Chip has established itself as a premier semiconductor design service provider in Southeast Asia. Our team of highly skilled engineers and designers are passionate about pushing the boundaries of what's possible in semiconductor technology.

Based in Penang, Malaysia, we serve clients worldwide with innovative solutions that combine deep technical expertise with a commitment to excellence. Our strategic location allows us to bridge the gap between Eastern manufacturing capabilities and Western design methodologies.
